This page is a work in progress more than any page of this website! Degoogling ain't easy at all and doesn't happen overnight. But I find joy in the process as I realize how much more cozy my devices now are. It made me more attentive. There's some beauty in replacing one app with a bunch that I cannot yet describe. Mayhaps the smaller and more specific a tool is, the more it is thoughtful and genuine? Human-scale, that is, I guess.


Successful

Google Alternative
Play Store F-Droid with additional repos (IzzyOnDroid, Bitwarden, Thunderbird)
Photos Fossify Gallery + Open Video Editor. Personally I don't need a cross-platform app and sync here, I transfer photos via USB. I never relied on syncing, because I'm annoyed I don't have full control over how files present themselves (I love folders and bulk renaming with FastStone Image Viewer)
Pay (Wallet?) Catima
Calendar Literally a physical calendar hanging on the wall
Files Material Files
Gboard HeliBoard


Partially successful, still needs work

Google Alternative Note
Keep Nextcloud (Murena as a provider) + Quillpad (Android) and Notepad++ with MarkdownViewer++ plugin (PC) I was initially looking for a cross-platform app, but realized I could just sync .txt files instead.

Murena sucks though and is constantly unavailable, rendering syncronization impossible. I guess I'll have to learn how to self-host
Drive Nextcloud, ProtonDrive Alternatives don't offer much storage, so I can't put a lot of stuff there. Currently use them to transfer small things from PC to smartphone and vice versa, and backup crucial files
Gmail ProtonMail, Thunderbird (PC), K-9 (Android) It's a bummer PM doesn't let users to use their bridge and PC app without a subscription.

I basically use Gmail for signing up and not sending emails, so maybe it's fine as long as it's just a spam mail storage, lol.
Docs and Sheets LibreOffice (offline, PC) Can't find an alternative, especially an ecosystem type, that doesn't suck ass visually AND can sync files across my devices
Chrome Brave, sometimes TOR, Opera (Android) Not satisfied that I'm still on chromium-based browsers
Search engine DuckDuckGo + uBlacklist add-on, Marginalia Have to occasionally use Yandex (yet another big tech corpo‚Ä¶) for searching by image. It's damn good at it.

Also I hate DuckDuckGo shoving the AI assistant down my throat
