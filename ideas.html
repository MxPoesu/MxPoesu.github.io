Ways to collaborate

  • Trading. I like when people draw me in the way they personally see me, letting their imagination loose. If you think it'd fit for me to be a purple dragon, draw that purple dragon! And I would reciprocate with anything you'd like me to draw that isn't in my 'dont's'.
  • 'Draw This In Your Style' challenge. And I mean it when I say 'challenge'!
  • Cover art for music albums. I'm a metalhead, and I particularly enjoy post-metal, atmospheric black, and folk, but also some post-rock too. If you're a small band or artist beginning their journey, we could work together.

Please only use email to communicate with me, that will ensure reliable communication: mx.poesu@proton.me
