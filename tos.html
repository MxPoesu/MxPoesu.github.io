Terms of Service

2-minute read


By commissioning an artwork you agree to the terms of service stated below.


DONT'S:

  • 🚫 minors.
  • 🚫 severe gore.
  • 🚫 sketches (I only do fully coloured artworks).
  • 🚫 imitation of someone else's style.
  • 🚫 anything having to do with AI or NFT.

Everything else goes (most likely).


Please only use email to commission me, that will ensure reliable communication.


50% PAID UPFRONT. The rest is paid before I send you a scan of the finished artwork. Don't send me the payment before I've requested you to do so. Otherwise I'll consider it a donation.


REVISIONS:

  • 📃 rough sketch: unlimited, if general idea doesn't change.
  • ✏️ pencil lineart: 2 revisions for free, only insignificant edits allowed; further revisions charged from $1 depending on the amount of work needed.
  • 🖼️ colouring and acrylic lineart: not allowed, because edits at this stage are either too difficult, or impossible since I'm a traditional artist.

SHIPPING price is not included and may vary depending on your location and size of the artwork.


COMMERCIAL USE negotiable. By default no commercial use rights are given. I retain all rights over the artwork, including the right to post it publicly. I don't do private commissions that I'm not allowed to publish on my own, but I can put the post on hold if your commission is a gift for someone. You are free to post the final artwork publicly.


NO GENERATIVE AI used or will ever be used at any stage of my art process, including conception, references, processing, etc. I will not accept AI-generated text or 'art' as a reference. I prohibit usage of my art in any proximity to generative AI and NFTs.
