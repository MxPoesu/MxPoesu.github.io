3-minute read


As long as you credit me and have no commercial incentive, you are free to:

When crediting me, linking to this website is preferred.


I prohibit the usage of my art in any proximity to generative AI and NFTs.


For commissioners

By commissioning an artwork you agree to the terms of service stated below.

Everything else goes (most likely).


Please only use email to commission me, that will ensure reliable communication: mx.poesu@proton.me


50% PAID UPFRONT. The rest is paid before I send you a scan of the finished artwork. Don't send me the payment before I've requested you to do so. Otherwise I'll consider it a donation.


REVISIONS:

NO GENERATIVE AI used or will ever be used at any stage of my art process, including conception, references, processing, etc. I will not accept AI-generated text or 'art' as a reference.
