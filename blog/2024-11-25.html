Re: Things that bring me joy

2-minute read


James wrote a post about things that bring him joy, so I figured I give that a try too!


I love sitting on the riverbank and gazing at the sparkling waters.


I love the feeling of freedom my bicycle gives me. It is a special kind of freedom â€” the one that is also safe and carefree.


I love volunteering and helping people whenever I can.


I love my girlfriend and making her happy. I knows she loves it when I take photos for her so I don't miss an opportunity to do just that. And I admire that both of us are polyamorous, nonbinary, and neurodivergent. It's beautiful and empowering.


My favourite colour is pink. It's soothing.


I love decluttering and sorting things. Lists and spreadsheets are awesome. It's as if I'm sorting out stuff in my brain, too.


I love to build my website and learn new things in HTML and CSS.


I love to play a carefree videogame, usually walking simulators and simple puzzles fall into this category.


I love physical activity, be that cleaning the house, hiking, or working as a courier.


Here's my favourite song that calms me down and alleviates my shutdowns.


I treasure my drawings.


I love to shave and feel the smoothness of my skin.


I love my fluffy rascals and taking care of them.


I love how green my town is. Nature is all around. There're swamps, lakes, mountains, rivers, and all kinds of picturesque landscapes.


I love traveling and seeing new places.


I love live concerts. When the organ plays everything trembles and you feel all the vibrations go through your body, it's invigorating.


I love gathering with friends and cooking together.


I love trains and all sorts of public transit, in spite of my occassional motion sickness.
